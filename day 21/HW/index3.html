BMW E46 M3 CS

The BMW E46 M3 CS is a rare and special edition of the iconic E46 M3, one of the most celebrated performance cars from BMW’s M division. Produced in limited numbers between 2004 and 2006, the M3 CS (Club Sport) offers a more focused, track-oriented version of the already highly capable M3. It was designed for enthusiasts who wanted a sharper driving experience without sacrificing too much of the day-to-day usability.

The E46 M3 CS features a number of enhancements over the standard M3, both in terms of performance and design. The exterior is distinguished by exclusive features such as a unique front splitter, wider front fenders, and lightweight 19-inch forged wheels, which not only improve aesthetics but also reduce overall weight. The CS model also gets special "Interlagos Blue" or "Silver Grey" paint finishes, adding a touch of exclusivity to the car’s striking look.

Inside, the E46 M3 CS is fitted with lightweight, manually adjustable sport seats that save weight compared to the standard electrically adjustable ones. The interior features high-quality materials like leather and alcantara, with a no-frills, driver-focused layout that emphasizes the connection between the driver and the machine.

  1. Model Name: BMW E46 M3 CS (Club Sport)
  2. Production Years: 2004 - 2006
  3. Limited Production: ~1,000 units worldwide
  4. Body Style: 2-Door Coupe
  5. Engine: 3.2L Inline-6 (S54)
